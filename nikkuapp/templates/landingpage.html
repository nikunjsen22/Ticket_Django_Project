How quickly will I get a response?

Most tickets receive an initial response within 1-2 hours during business hours. For priority issues, our response time is even faster — typically within 30 minutes.

Can I use SupportHub for my business?

Absolutely! SupportHub is designed for both individual users and businesses of all sizes. We offer scalable plans that grow with your needs.

Is there a limit to how many tickets I can submit?

Free accounts can submit up to 5 tickets per month. Our paid plans offer unlimited ticket submissions to accommodate all your support needs.
