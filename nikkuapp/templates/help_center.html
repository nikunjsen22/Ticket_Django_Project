Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new support ticket?
To create a new support ticket, click on the "New Ticket" button on the dashboard or tickets page. Fill in the required information including subject, description, and priority level, then submit the ticket.
How can I track my ticket status?
You can track your ticket status by visiting the Tickets page. Each ticket shows its current status, priority, and last update time. You'll also receive email notifications for any status changes.
What are the different ticket priorities?
We have four priority levels: Low, Medium, High, and Urgent. Low priority tickets are non-critical issues, while Urgent tickets require immediate attention. Choose the appropriate priority based on the impact and urgency of your issue.
